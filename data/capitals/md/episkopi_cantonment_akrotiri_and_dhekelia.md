**Episkopi Cantonment** ([Greek](/wiki/Greek_language "Greek language"): Φρουρά Επισκοπής, [Turkish](/wiki/Turkish_language "Turkish language"): *Episkopi Kantonu* ) is the [capital](/wiki/Capital_city "Capital city") of [Akrotiri and Dhekelia](/wiki/Akrotiri_and_Dhekelia "Akrotiri and Dhekelia"), a [British overseas territory](/wiki/British_Overseas_Territories "British Overseas Territories") on the island of [Cyprus](/wiki/Cyprus "Cyprus"), administered as a [military base](/wiki/Military_base "Military base"). It is located in the northwestern part of the Western Sovereign Base Area (Akrotiri), one of the two areas which comprise the territory. Although it is not the largest of the British [military](/wiki/Military "Military") bases on the island, it is home to both the civilian and military administration headquarters of the Sovereign Base Areas. Episkopi is the current command centre of [British Forces Cyprus](/wiki/British_Forces_Cyprus "British Forces Cyprus").^[\[1\]](#cite_note-1)^{#cite_ref-1}  

Etymology {#Etymology}
----------------------

\[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Episkopi_Cantonment&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Etymology")\]

The word 'Episkopi' in Episkopi Cantonment's name comes from the Greek word Επισκοπικός meaning "Episcopal." The cantonment was named so due to the site previously serving as the bishop's seat of an Orthodox diocese.^[\[2\]](#cite_note-2)^{#cite_ref-2}  

Transportation {#Transportation}
--------------------------------

\[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Episkopi_Cantonment&action=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Transportation")\]

Paved motorways and other smaller roads connect the cantonment area with the rest of Akrotiri and Dhekelia.
